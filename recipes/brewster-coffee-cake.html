Brewster's Coffee Cake

Description

A cake that could only have been made by a pigeon as perfect as Brewster himself, Brewster's Coffee cake is a delight to the senses. With lush tones of chocolate, grape, raisins, and birdseed, Brewster's coffee cake is sure to delight cake lovers and coffee drinkers alike.

Ingredients

  • .5 lbs of ground coffee
  • .5 lbs of bird seed
  • Cake Mix

Steps

  1. Put the ground coffee and bird seed in a bird house.
  2. Pay the gyroid outside the birdhouse approximately 1200 bells.
  3. Wait until the following day. Consume the cake mix if you get hungry
  4. Inside the birdhouse will be your coffee cake!

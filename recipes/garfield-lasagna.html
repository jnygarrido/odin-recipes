Garfield Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious recipe featuring the real lasagna that Garfield actually ate in the comic strip garfield. Garfield lasagna is known for being filling, savory, and heavy, leaving some who eat the dish ready to take a nap - just like the famous cat himself!

Please note that Garfield was not harmed in the creation of this dish.

Ingredients

  • Two lasagna noodles, Large flavor
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Beef Mixture (Anti-beef mixture for a vegetarian option)
  • Catnip Extract

Steps

  1. Boil the tomato sauce to a medium heat.
  2. In a large pan, combine the beef mixture, warm tomato sauce, and catnip extract. Stir until crispy.
  3. Places the crispy mix in between the large flavored lasagna noodles.
  4. Bake in the oven at 450 until golden brown or cat meows begin echoing from the oven, whichever comes first.

Scooby Snacks

Description

A delicious treat for man or man's best friend, Scooby snacks are guaranteed to give you the courage you need to face ghosts, ghouls, or goblins on any day of the week!

Ingredients

  • Bacon Strips(2)
  • Courage
  • Your Favorite Dog Food

Steps

  1. Wait for a murder to be committed.
  2. When a murder is committed, do not call the authorities. Instead, form a gang of friends dedicated to solving the crime, using your Courage to over come the rumors of ghosts, ghouls, and goblins.
  3. Unveil the culprit behind the murder - it was old man Jenkins!
  4. Eat the bacon strips, and give the dog food to your dog.
